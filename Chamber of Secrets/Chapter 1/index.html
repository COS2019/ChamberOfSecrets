Welcome one and all to the Chamber of Secrets. The place where your grey matter shall be tested with questions all amusing and tricky. Think yourself smart enough, then follow up, alone or in teams of max 3.

Following general rules apply :
  1. The current level number is indicated at the top
  2. Answers to the series of questions are obvious and may not need the help of a search engine
  3. Some stages are not puzzles rather choices to make to proceed further. Each choice reveals a different question!
  4. The answer can be a small word, a string of characters or numbers. Sometimes it can be more than one word
  5. Once you have the answer, convert it into lowercase and remove all the spaces
  6. To proceed to the next stage, replace the part in the URL before the .html with the answer Like: chamberofsecrets.000webhostapp.com/theanswer.html
  7. Remember, there can only be one winner! So helping out your fellow mates(rivals?) may not be a good choice!
  8. After crossing each level, you must take a screenshot! And then in the end mail these screenshots to : chamberofsecrets.nits@gmail.com
  9. Fill up this doc before proceeding any further!

So, Swish and Flick...
